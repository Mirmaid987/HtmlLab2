Reasons I love Lancaster

I love Lancaster because it is a good blend of city living and country living. One moment you can be in the city, and the next, you can be riding in a horse and buggy.
I like how close we are to the beach and the mountains. There is something for everyone here.

Here are some pros and cons about living in Lancaster, Pennsylvania

Here are some Pros of Lancaster

  • It doesnt take you very long to get to a grocery store or gas station.
  • Lancaster has some great coffee. Square One Coffee is made in Lancaster and shipped all around the state.
  • Lastly, you can get really fresh and organic produce right under your nose

Cons of Lancaster

  • Not many vegan places to eat, however, you can make it work
  • The people here are not the most open minded, you got the few people though
  • The last con about Lancaster is that we can get some harsh winters, and you have to shovel!!

Living in Lancaster really is not bad. People always think I am amish. However, believe it or not, not everyone in Lancaster is amish!!
